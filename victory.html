Victory Park

Victory park is on Pershing Ave, Stockton, CA. Victory has beautiful scenery and is home to the Haggin Museum. There are always activities at Victory which draws a lot of families making the park a safe place for the community. This park also has a firefighter department within the park. Victory has a nice pond with geese and a playground for children to play at. There are also tennis courts, a basketball court, and a baseball field. There is so much land and a free grass arena to have a picnic. Victory park really unites the community with all the attractions it has.
